About Me


Welcome ! I am a published childrens book author and illustrator, just graduated in July 2023 for a Masters Degree in Indie Game Development, and currently studying for a career as a Full Stack Developer with Codecademy.

Throughout the masters, I explored most areas of game development, and learn't many different applications, Adobe Animate, Character Animator, and Premiere-Pro. I also worked with 3dsMax, Maya and Blender's animation 3D modeling software.

However after, I was eager to learn about creating interactive elements, for applications, and web development. I started studying to be a full stack engineer, with Codecademy in May 2023, just before graduation, and have been consistantly practicing with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python and GitHub.

I am extremely motivated and enthusiastic about my career path ahead of me, and can't wait to immerse myself, in the world of web development.
